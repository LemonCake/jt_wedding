Bridesmaids
Amy Hoang
Matron of Honor. Tina has known Amy since living in the same dorms freshman year at UC Davis. Amy's unique character at the time caught Tina's attention and she found her very entertaining. Throughout the college years, she popped Tina's "bubble" and taught her many life lessons.
Wendy Hwang
Tina's sister. Wendy knows everything about Tina that a sister would.
Annie Wu
Post-college, Tina met Annie coincidentally at a mutual friend's bowling event. They share many similar interests, especially eating and traveling. They both love all things Taiwan (woot!)
Esther Hui
Senior year at Davis, Tina decided to join Alpha Phi Omega, a community service fraternity, and was matched with Esther as her "big brother" because of their similar personality, hobbies, and interests. They've enjoyed crafting and baking together throughout the years.
Jenn Chen
Tina also met Jenn in the dorms freshman year at UC Davis. Jenn and Amy were roommates and she is just as funny. Like Amy, Jenn also had a hand in bringing Tina up to speed with the real world.
Groomsmen
Benny Wong
Best man and best friend. Benny and Josh met each other in graduate school 30 minutes before a midterm. Benny never told Josh his score. They have been with each other since. Moving to Chicago, and then to SF. Many joke that they are inseparable, and they are right.
John Wu
Josh's younger brother. While quite different in character, shared much of the same hobbies growing up. One may think of Josh as the mentor, but the truth is they learn a lot from each other. They are as close as brothers can be.
Antonio Wang
Everyone has that one best friend they grew up with. Antonio has been that person for 25 years. Although thousands of miles apart now, still talk with each other on Skype for hours at a time.
Chris Liu
They met each other in a Chicago summer on a night out. Before they knew it, they spent a majority of their drunken nights in each other's presence over the years following. Josh considers Chris one of the few friends that know him well.
Ike Song
Ike is the friend that Josh wished he had met earlier. They met on a Halloween weekend in NYC, and have been buddies since. They don't see each other much, but when they do it's always a good time.
